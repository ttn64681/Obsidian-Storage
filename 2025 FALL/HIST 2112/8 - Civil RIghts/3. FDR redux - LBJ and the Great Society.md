### c)  VISTA

### d) Community Action Programs
- *one of the big failures of Great Society*
- Example of **"Good Idea gone Horribly Wrong"**

- Goal: **Make Middle-Class people less arrogant**
- Context:
	- Progressive were a little arrogant and nativist
	- "I'm here from the Middle-Class to turn you into me"
	- CAP wanted to make people less arrogant

- **Govt gives money to area like Atlanta to help City Govt fix stuff in city**
- Normally, Govt give that money to City Govt to help them fix stuff
- **They set up advisory board w/ poor people to decide how that money is spent**
	- **Give the poor people a voice**

- DOES NOT WORK THAT WAY
- City Governments are upset that people tell them how to spend their money
	- They wanna build highway and stadiums to bring in tourists
- Poor people in neighborhood are actually really upset b/c, despite having a voice, they say that **"Money is NOT THE PROBLEM"**
- The problem is **"WE HAVE NO POWER IN CITY GOVERNMENT!!!!"**

- Got so contentious and ugly that **they shut the program down**
