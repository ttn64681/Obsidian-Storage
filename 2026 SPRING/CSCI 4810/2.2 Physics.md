# Distance, Velocity, Acceleration
- s is distance
- v0 is init vel
- t is time
- a is accel

s = vo t
- distance w/ constant v
s = vo t + 1/2a t^2
- distance w/ velocity + acceleration
v = vo + at
- velocity change w/ constant accel
