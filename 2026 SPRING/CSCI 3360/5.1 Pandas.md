- Selecting individual columns:
	- can't do complaint[5]\['Illegal Parking'] -> error
	- instead use loc or iloc
- **loc vs iloc:**
    - `.loc` (label-based): Selects by row and column labels/names.
    - `.iloc` (integer-based): Selects by integer position (index) of rows and columns.
    - **Reason for error `complaint[5]['Illegal Parking']`:** Direct indexing `df[row_index][column_label]` in Pandas can be ambiguous and is not the recommended way to select both rows and columns simultaneously. It often leads to `SettingWithCopyWarning` or unexpected behavior. Use `.loc` or `.iloc` for robust and clear row/column selection.
